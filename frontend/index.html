Project Goals

RoadMap

Short summary of our Roadmap.

Phase 1

-Brawly Bears Launch:

The official launch of the Brawly Bears NFT project, announcing the release of the 10,000 images featuring bears equipped with a variety of weapons and accessories.

-Open Minting:

The minting process is open to the public, allowing anyone to purchase a Brawly Bears NFT for 0.035 ETH. Minting will continue until all 10,000 NFTs are sold.

-Marketing and Collaborations:

Aggressive marketing campaigns are launched to promote the project across various channels, including social media, NFT marketplaces, and crypto communities. Collaborations with other NFT projects and artists are pursued to increase awareness and exposure for Brawly Bears.

-Random Reward Raffle:

As part of our commitment to building a strong community, we will hold a random reward raffle exclusively for members of the Brawly Bears community. This raffle will give members a chance to win exclusive Brawly Bears merchandise or even a Brawly Bears NFT.

Don't miss out on this opportunity to own a piece of the Brawly Bears world. Get yours now before they're all gone!

Phase 2

-Community Engagement:

Brawly Bears team will focus on building and engaging with its community by hosting social media campaigns, AMAs (Ask-Me-Anything), and other community events through our Discord server and different social media channels.

-Partnerships and Integrations:

The team will explore potential partnerships and integrations with other projects, such as gaming or virtual reality projects that could showcase Brawly Bears as well as enhance its value and appeal.

-Secondary Market:

The Brawly Bears NFTs will be tradable on secondary markets, and the team will actively monitor and support these marketplaces to ensure liquidity and transparency.

-Random Reward for Holders:

We believe in rewarding our loyal holders, so we will be holding a series of random reward events exclusively for Brawly Bears NFT holders. These rewards may include exclusive merchandise, rare Brawly Bears NFTs, or other exciting prizes. Holders will be automatically entered into these events, so the more Brawly Bears NFTs you hold, the higher your chances of winning.

Phase 3

-Reveal Tokens and Rarity:

After the initial minting period is over, we will reveal the unique characteristics and rarity of each Brawly Bear NFT. This will include details such as the number of layers and colors, as well as information about rare attributes and traits. This information will help Brawly Bear NFT holders understand the unique value of their NFTs.

-Market Enhancement:

The Brawly Bears team will continue to monitor and enhance the secondary market experience for holders, including features like price history, volume tracking, and market insights.

-Stackers Reward Program:

We will be introducing a Stackers Reward Program. This program will incentivize holders who stack multiple Brawly Bears NFTs, with rewards such as exclusive merchandise, access to future drops, and more.

-Community Engagement:

The Brawly Bears team will continue to engage with its community through regular updates, contests, and other events. We will also explore new ways to create value for our holders and support the wider NFT ecosystem.

Phase 4

-Future Development:

With the initial phase completed, the team will explore new development opportunities and create new collections under the Brawly Bears brand. We are cooking the Brawly Fists project, a new token focused on unique fighting abilities and weapons of each Brawly Bear

-Continued Community Support:

As always, the Brawly Bears team remains committed to supporting and engaging with our community. We will continue to explore new ways to create value for our holders, including exclusive events and rewards, as well as partnerships and collaborations with other projects in the NFT space.

-Brawly Bears Gen 2:

Building on the success of Brawly Bears, we are excited to announce the creation of Brawly Bears Gen 2. This new generation of Brawly Bears NFTs will feature even more layers, attributes, and rare characteristics, as well as new and improved fighting abilities and weapons. We are committed to delivering a new and exciting NFT experience for our community, and we can't wait to share more details about Brawly Bears Gen 2 soon.
